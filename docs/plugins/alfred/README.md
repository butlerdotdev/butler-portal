# Alfred

:::info Coming Soon
Alfred is under active development and not yet available.
:::

Alfred is an infrastructure knowledge platform. It indexes your documentation, runbooks, and incident history to provide contextual answers about your infrastructure. When you ask a question, Alfred searches across your indexed sources and returns relevant information along with links to the original documents.

Alfred is designed to reduce context-switching during incident response and day-to-day operations. Instead of searching across wikis, runbooks, and past incident reports manually, you query Alfred from within the Portal and receive answers grounded in your organization's own knowledge base.
